Tutorial

  1. Signing in: To play the game you must sign in with a google account. Simply press the sign in with google account button and you are set.
  2. Local Game: To play a game with someone on the same computer press the Local button.
    This will redirect you to a game session.
  3. Remote Game: To play a game with someone over the network press the Find Remote Game buton.
    You will be automatically matched with another player over the network.
  4. Logout: To log out of your account you press the Logout button.
  5. The end! Press the pulsing logo at the top of the page to return to the main page.
